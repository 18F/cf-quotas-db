Individual Quotas

Quotas

  • /api/quotas/
  • /api/quota/*guid*/

This endpoint also have the "since" and "until" date parameters.

If the "until" parameter is not present the date will default to today UTC.

Examples

  • ex. /api/quotas/?since=2013
  • ex. /api/quotas/*guid*/?since=2013&until=2014
